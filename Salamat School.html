Our Mission

Mission Of Our Schools is dedicated to inspiring a passion for learning and developing the attitudes, skills and knowledge that will enable all students to maximize their potential as positive, responsible participants in our democratic society and the global community.



Aim and objective

Our Objective is to prepare students for the Secondary school leaving certificate examination of Karnataka state. And encouraging, preparing students to participate in the various curricular and co-curricular activities both in the school and the inter-school competitions.



Motto

A motto is rule of life adopted by a family or institution. Our motto is LET YOUR LIGHT SHINE. The motto sums up very briefly the vision of our school – to enable our students to be lights of the world through their day to day life at home,in the school,in the neighbourhood and wherever they go

We are a school with a difference! We value individualism, creativity and innovation and strive to nurture them in our students. Our motto “Education for all” is at the heart of everything we do at the school. Our vision for the St.Xavier’s Chain of Schools is that “We will provide the best education in the subcontinent. To attain this vision, the school is committed to continuous improvement with goals, strategies, policies and procedures in the school’s progress. We aim to not just impart knowledge to the students, but also to inculcate in them – wisdom, compassion and a humanitarian spirit. We have a multi-cultural student population; hence we teach children the importance of tolerance and respecting each others culture. Discipline, values and integrity are the very foundation of this school.
